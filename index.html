Assessing the effect of Emerging Tchnologies on customer satisfaction in Supply-Chain Management Practices.

Customer satisfaction is a critical metric in business performance, particularly in supply chain management (SCM). As supply chains become more complex and competitive, the integration of emerging technologies offers both opportunities and challenges to organizations aiming to improve customer service.

Emerging technologies like the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence (AI), big data analytics, and blockchain are significantly impacting customer satisfaction in supply chain management by enhancing visibility, enabling real-time tracking, optimizing delivery times, personalizing experiences, and improving overall operational efficiency, leading to a more positive customer experience.

Emerging technologies can improve customer experience by giving businesses a better understanding of customers, by increasing connectivity, and by empowering customers through better use of self-service there-by acheiving customer satisfaction.

Research Problem

The integration of emerging technologies in supply chain management has led to numerous improvements, such as improved product tracking, faster delivery times, and more accurate demand forecasting. However, there remains a gap in understanding how these technologies directly affect customer satisfaction. While some studies highlight the operational advantages of technology adoption, few focus on its influence on the end-customer's experience and satisfaction. Thus, the research problem is to examine the effects of emerging technologies on customer satisfaction in supply chain management. This includes understanding how technological innovations impact the delivery of goods and services, responsiveness to customer needs, and the overall customer experience.

Research Aims

1. To assess how emerging technologies influence key aspects of customer satisfaction in supply chain management, such as product quality, delivery speed, communication, and service reliability.

2. To assess customer perceptions of technology-driven supply chain improvements, such as automated inventory management, predictive analytics, real-time tracking, and blockchain transparency.

3. To identify challenges and barriers in the adoption of emerging technologies that could negatively impact customer satisfaction.

Research Questions

1. How do emerging technologies impact customer satisfaction in supply chain management?

2. How do technological innovations such as AI, IoT, and blockchain influence customer expectations and perceptions of supply chain performance?

3. What are the key customer satisfaction factors that are positively or negatively impacted by the adoption of emerging technologies in the supply chain?

4. How can companies address the challenges of technology adoption to sustain or enhance customer satisfaction?

Emerging technologies are technologies whose development, practical applications, or both are still largely unrealized. These technologies are generally new but also include old technologies finding new applications. Emerging technologies are often perceived as capable of changing the status quo.

Emerging technologies are characterized by radical novelty (in application even if not in origins), relatively fast growth, coherence, prominent impact, and uncertainty and ambiguity. In other words, an emerging technology can be defined as "a radically novel and relatively fast growing technology characterised by a certain degree of coherence persisting over time and with the potential to exert a considerable impact on the socio-economic domain(s) which is observed in terms of the composition 0f actors, institutions and patterns of interactions among those, along with the associated knowledge production processes. Its most prominent impact, however, lies in the future and so in the emergence phase is still somewhat uncertain and ambiguous."

Ways emerging technologies improve customer satisfaction in supply chain management:

1. Self-Service Portals

User-friendly digital platforms empower customers to take control of their experience. These self-service portals provide convenient and efficient ways to improve satisfaction by enabling users to do the following:

1. Find answers: Extensive knowledge bases, searchable FAQs, and interactive tutorials equip customers with the information they need to troubleshoot issues or answer basic questions themselves.

2. Manage Accounts:Secure online portals enable users to view account information, update details, make payments, and manage subscriptions independently.

3. Resolve Issues: Online forms, chatbots, and troubleshooting guides empower customers to diagnose problems. They can use the knowledge to potentially resolve common issues without needing to contact customer service representatives directly.

2. Customer Service Gamification

Companies can transform customer service by incorporating game-like elements. It'll foster a more engaging and rewarding experience for customers and service representatives. Imagine that completing a customer satisfaction survey is no longer a chore. It can be an opportunity to earn points towards a virtual badge or unlock exclusive discounts.

3. AI-Powered Customer Service

AI can anticipate needs and proactively offer solutions to customers. For example, an AI-powered chat window can pop up to provide personalized recommendations based on the customer's browsing history. You could even use it to suggest laptops with features similar to those in previous searches. This proactive approach improves customer satisfaction by saving them time and effort when looking for a laptop by themselves. It also creates opportunities for relevant upselling and cross-selling, increasing sales without feeling pushy. By addressing these concerns before they turn into problems, AI can also significantly reduce the need for reactive customer service interventions, leading to cost savings for the business.
Note: guidde is one of the companies at the forefront of AI innovation, offering tools to create personalized video documentation and training materials. This approach reduces reliance on manual knowledge base building and creates a satisfying customer journey.

4. AI-Driven Data Analytics

The powerful combination of AI and data analytics is transforming how businesses understand their customers. Companies can use it to get insights from a variety of sources, such as transactional histories, website behavior, and social media interactions.Note: AI-driven data analytics has a substantial impact. According to PWC, organizations that use this approach to make decisions can outperform their competitors by 6% in profitability and 5% in productivity.

5. Chatbot and Virtual-assistant

Chatbots can deliver immediate responses to frequently asked customer inquiries, streamlining the resolution process. They can answer questions about order status, returns, or business hours. They also guide users through complex processes step-by-step, like resetting a password or scheduling an appointment. This flexibility can improve task completion rates. Plus, chatbots are available 24/7, which can be especially helpful for customers in different time zones.While automation is a crucial benefit, chatbots can also contribute to a more personalized customer experience by remembering a user's past interactions and tailoring responses accordingly. This can make people feel valued and understood.
